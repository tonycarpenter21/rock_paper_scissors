How To Play:

Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors!

  1. Start by selecting either "Classic Game" or "Advanced Game" on the home screen.
  2. Click a card you think will beat the computer's choice.
  3. If you're correct, you win!

Classic Game Rules:

You will have the choice of three options and simply click your choice to play.

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats Paper

Advanced Game Rules:

You will have the choice of five options and simply click your choice to play.

  • Rock beats Scissors and Lizard
  • Paper beats Rock and Spock
  • Scissors beats Paper and Lizard
  • Lizard beats Spock and Paper
  • Spock beats Scissors and Rock
